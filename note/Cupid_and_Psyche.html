    # Cupid and Psyche

    There was a king with three daughters, the youngest daughter Psyche was famous for its beauty. Venus is the goddess whose functions encompassed love, beauty, She was jealous of the beauty of Psyche, then she ordered Cupid to punish Psyche. However, Cupid fell in love with Psyche when he saw Psyche, and let her be his wife and hides her in a shrine, every night, Cupid came to stay with Psyche, but, Psyche couldn't see Cupid and could only hear his voice. Cupid said:"don't ask who I am and don't wonder what I look like." Psyche promised.

    Later, the two sisters of Psyche came to the shrine, they were very jealous of the wealthy life of Psyche, they incited Psyche went to peek at the Cupid's look at night, so Psyche picked up the oil lamp and saw Cupid's face. Because of Psyche break her promise, Cupid was very angry. withdrew everything that was given to Psyche, arranged a lot of difficult and dangerous tasks for her.

    The last task was to went to hell to take a box. Psyche had overcome a lot of difficulties and successfully got the box. On the way back, curiosity drove Psyche to open the box, the god of sleep was in the box, making Psyche sleep. Finally Cupid forgave Psyche and made Psyche woke up. Venus was moved by her love for Cupid, allowing her to be with Cupid, it also touched the gods, so Psyche was sealed as a goddess, never die, can be with Cupid forever.
